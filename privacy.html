Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Wildly Curious ("we", "our", "the app", also referred to as "Nursery Pipeline Uploader" in developer settings) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our content publishing tool, including its integration with TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram.

1. Who we are

Wildly Curious is an independently operated content channel and internal publishing tool used to prepare and schedule short-form video content for social media platforms.

2. What information we access

When you connect your TikTok, YouTube, or Instagram account to this tool, we access only the permissions you explicitly authorize during the OAuth consent process, which may include:

  • The ability to upload and publish video content on your behalf
  • Basic account/profile information necessary to identify the connected account
  • Read-only access to content and analytics you have authorized, where applicable

We do not access your private messages, contacts, or any data unrelated to video publishing.

3. How we use this information

Information accessed through connected accounts is used exclusively to:

  • Upload and schedule video content that you have created and approved
  • Retrieve publishing status and basic performance metrics for your own review

We do not sell, rent, or share this information with third parties. We do not use this data for advertising or profiling.

4. Data storage

Authentication tokens are stored locally and securely, and are used solely to authorize publishing actions you initiate. Video content and associated metadata are stored locally until published.

5. Data retention and deletion

You may revoke this app's access to your TikTok, YouTube, or Instagram account at any time through that platform's own account settings. Doing so immediately terminates our ability to access your account. You may also request deletion of any locally stored data by contacting us using the details below.

6. Third-party services

This tool integrates with third-party platforms (TikTok, YouTube/Google, Instagram/Meta, and AI service providers used solely for content generation). Each of these platforms has its own privacy policy governing their handling of your data.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: wildlycuriousfacts@gmail.com
